Dementia Awareness Hub - Website Plan

Site Name

Dementia Awareness Hub
This name was selected because it clearly represents the organization's purpose: to raise awareness, provide education, and support individuals and families in Malawi affected by dementia.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide reliable information about dementia, educate communities in Malawi, support caregivers, reduce stigma, and offer ways for visitors to connect with the organization for resources or partnerships.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find information about dementia symptoms and early signs?
  • How can I contact the organization for caregiver support or volunteer opportunities?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #691004 - Used for headings, navigation, and buttons.
Secondary Color: #D9E6F2 - Used for backgrounds and highlight areas.

Typography

Heading Font: Arial, bold - Used for all page titles and section headers.
Body Font: Arial, regular - Used for paragraphs and descriptive text.
